About Me

I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. My supervisors are Christian Blohmann and Peter Teichner. My research interests are in higher differential geometry and functorial field theory.

Check out the new Quantum topology seminar!

Together with David Prinz and Peter Teichner we organized the Physical Mathematics Seminar. Recently, I organized the second BGW PhD retreat.
