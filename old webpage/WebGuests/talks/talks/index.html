Publications

  • Functoriality of bornological groupoid convolution with Christian Blohmann, preprint (submitted), arXiv

  • My Master’s thesis: Cohomological extension of groupoid convolution (2023)
    Supervised by Christian Blohmann. I use the convenient setting of bornological vector spaces to prove Morita invariance of the smooth convolution algebra. Contents partially incorporated and superceded by our paper Functoriality of bornological groupoid convolution.
  • My Bachelor’s thesis: The noncommutative geometry of symplectic singularities (2020)
    Supervised by Christian Blohmann. Some singularities of symplectic forms can be resolved by Dirac structures. In the example of the magnetic monopole I integrate the resulting Lie algebroid and compute the convolution C*-algebra of the Lie groupoid.
